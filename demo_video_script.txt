Demo video outline (3 to 5 minutes)

1. Start with the project folder
- Show the files: app.py, train_models.py, README.md, models, notebooks, reports, and sample_docs.
- Mention that labels are 0 for Human and 1 for AI.

2. Run the app
- In the terminal, run: streamlit run app.py
- Open the local Streamlit link.

3. Test a human-written sample
- Upload or paste sample_docs/human_sample.txt.
- Choose SVM first.
- Point out the prediction, AI detection score, human score, and confidence.

4. Test an AI-written sample
- Upload or paste sample_docs/ai_sample.txt.
- Use SVM again.
- Show that the AI detection score is much higher.

5. Show model comparison
- Scroll to the side-by-side model comparison.
- Mention that the same text is tested against all six models.

6. Show notebook/report results
- Open notebooks/project1_notebook.ipynb or reports/model_comparison.csv.
- Briefly explain that SVM was the best saved model, followed by AdaBoost, FNN, and CNN.

7. Download report
- Click Download analysis report in the app.
- Mention that the report includes the chosen model, scores, text statistics, and model comparison.
